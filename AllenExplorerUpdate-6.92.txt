Allen Explorer v6.92 Update Log

Fixed: When the window is maximized, dragging the title bar cannot restore to the normal window.
Optimized: The experience of dragging a tab to create a new window.
Optimized: The navigation bar buttons are refined into three buttons, and the visibility of each button can be set.
Optimized: The payment experience for members.
Optimized: The display effect of shortcut icons at low resolutions.
New: Settings for Start icons / Quick Launch icons.
New: Press the middle mouse wheel to open a folder in a new tab (anywhere).
Others: Performance and stability optimization.
